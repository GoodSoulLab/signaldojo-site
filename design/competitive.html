Competitive Landscape

The Heroic ecosystem gap, three tiers of adjacent competitors, and the strategic conclusion. Context for every positioning and messaging decision. Built from Phase 3 of the Personal Brand Strategy.

The Heroic Gap

Heroic trains coaches to coach. Not to practice.

Heroic has trained over 10,000 coaches from 100+ countries. The coaching certification includes community support and accountability — not a structured system for positioning, offer design, and consistent client acquisition. The gap: Heroic trains coaches to coach and facilitate. It does not train them to position, package, message, and acquire clients as independent practitioners. That gap is unclaimed. Entry is complementary, not competitive. The next step Heroic does not offer.

Source: Phase 3 — The Heroic Ecosystem Gap
Tier 1 — Closest to Wedge

No one owns this transition.

No player in the Heroic ecosystem owns the certified-to-practicing transition. This is the unclaimed territory. The competitive advantage is not differentiation from existing players — it is entry into a gap that does not yet have an occupant.

Source: Phase 3 — Tier 1
Tier 2 — LinkedIn-Native

Personal brand and positioning. No inner layer.

Justin Welsh: solopreneur brand building, broad audience, no inner development layer. Kait LeDonne: positioning for consultants and coaches, no somatic or identity layer. Jasmin Alic: LinkedIn growth and profile optimization, tactical, no transformation framework. Brand Builders Group (Rory Vaden): broad, premium-priced, not community-specific. None speak the Heroic language or hold the inner development layer.

Source: Phase 3 — Tier 2
Strategic Conclusion

A warm, identifiable, underserved pond.

None of these players speak the Heroic language, hold the inner development layer, or serve this specific certified community. This is not competing with Justin Welsh for a general LinkedIn audience. This is entering a warm, identifiable, underserved pond with existing credibility and shared vocabulary. Community proximity, shared language, and a built system that closes the exact gap Heroic does not address.

Source: Phase 3 — Strategic Conclusion
