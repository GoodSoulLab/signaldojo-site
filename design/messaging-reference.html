The complete messaging reference block for Signal Dojo Core. Headlines, key messaging angles, CTA language, proof and trust notes, and open hypotheses. Optimized for use in newsletter, post, carousel, video, and content waterfall prompts.

Headlines

Three proven options.

You Did the Inner Work. Here Is the Outer System. — Primary. You Finished Your Certification. Now What? — Secondary. The Gap Between Certified and Income-Generating Has a Name. It Has a Fix. — Tertiary. Each headline names the trigger state without judgment and implies a path forward without promising one prematurely.

Key Messaging Angles

Five angles. Use in rotation.

You do not have a motivation problem. You have a system problem. The certification prepared you to coach. It did not give you a go-to-market system. The inner work does not precede the outer work. They develop together. Later never arrives without a system. This landing page is one of the outputs — the founder ran the process on himself.
