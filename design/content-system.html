Content System Map

The Signal Engine input layer, five content pillars, storytelling formats, content waterfall, three-lever framework, and the mental model that governs the entire system. Built from Phase 9 of the Personal Brand Strategy.

Signal Engine

Input before output.

Signal sources feed everything downstream: Reddit, Substack, YouTube, X, Threads, Bluesky, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, and Real Life. Raw signal is captured in a Signal Log in Notion, tagged by phrase, type, source, URL, theme, and insight. Pattern Recognition runs weekly on Fridays. Validated Ideas emerge from that weekly pass and feed the newsletter. Nothing gets published without passing through this layer first.

Source: Phase 9 — Signal Engine
Five Content Pillars

The gap. The transition. The system. The inner foundation. The return.

Pillar 1: The gap — capable vs actual, 14-year obsession, north star theme. Pillar 2: The transition — certified to income-generating, sharpest hook. Pillar 3: The system — Signal Dojo, product thinking, three levers. Pillar 4: The inner foundation — self-trust, identity, somatic layer. Pillar 5: The return — rupture, repair, what comes back stronger.

Source: Phase 9 — Five Content Pillars
Content Waterfall

Write once. System distributes.

Tuesday 8:24am ET cornerstone piece on Substack is the keystone. From it flows: posts on X, Threads, Bluesky (1-3 per day), carousels on LinkedIn and Instagram (1-2 per week), short videos on TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts (3-4 per week), YouTube and Podcast (1 per week). Posts can also inspire future newsletters, closing the feedback loop back to the keystone layer.

Source: Phase 9 — Content Waterfall
Mental Model

Reference. Content. Execution. Feedback Loop.

Reference Layer: who I am, who I help, what I offer. Content Layer: what I believe, what I am noticing, what I am teaching. Execution Layer: posts, carousels, videos. Feedback Loop: signal, reflection, refinement. Everything feeds forward. Strategy becomes content. Content becomes posts. Posts become insights. Insights become the next content.
